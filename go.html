Message from the Team


Attendees,
We hope you had a great time and learned a thing or two at our 4th Annual Project RISHI Leadership Conference. This was our largest conference yet and strongly believe that your engagement and participation will provide immediate upgrades in all aspects of RISHI in the coming months. In order for us to continue improving the conference each year, we would really appreciate the time for you to fill out this brief, anonymous survey and share your feedback on all aspects of the conference.
Finally, on behalf of the entire National Leadership team, we would like to thank you for both attending and for your commitment to Project RISHI. As iterated throughout the weekend, we set these conferences up to serve as a valuable resource to help you accomplish your goals. We truly do believe in your internal desires to make an impact and want to make sure we can help in any way we can to help you reach that goal.
Thanks again,
Uday Gulati
President
